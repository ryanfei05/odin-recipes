Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe


Description

Whether you're feeling under the weather or you're just in need of a little comfort, there's nothing like homemade chicken noodle soup to warm you up from the inside out. This short recipe will make fabulous tasting chicken noodle soup in just 40 minutes of preparation and cooking. Recipe taken from allrecipes.com

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 4 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can vegetable broth
  • ½ pound chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 ½ cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Voila! You have a perfect chicken noodle soup!
